# Maintainers

CalmStudio follows a **BDFL + Committers** governance model. The project lead
has final decision authority, with committers who have earned merge access
through sustained, high-quality contributions.

## How to Become a Committer

1. **Contribute regularly** — multiple merged PRs over several months
2. **Demonstrate quality** — clean code, thorough tests, good reviews
3. **Be a community member** — help others, review PRs, participate in discussions
4. **Be nominated** — any existing committer or the project lead can nominate

Nominations are discussed privately among existing committers. The project lead
makes the final decision.

## Responsibilities

All maintainers are expected to:

- Follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Review PRs within the [SLA defined in CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md#code-review-sla)
- Sign off commits with DCO (`git commit -s`)
- Participate in security vulnerability response when needed
